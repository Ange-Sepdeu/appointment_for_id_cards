Booking Appointment for National ID Card

Case Study: Cameroon

Procedure to Establish an Official National ID Card in Cameroon

  1. Step 1: Visit the nearest identification office or book online for an appointment

    Find the nearest office or use our online system to schedule an appointment at your convenience.

  2. Step 2: Provide the necessary documents

    • Birth Certificate
    • Proof of Residence
    • Previous ID card (if applicable)
    • Passport-size photographs

  3. Step 3: Complete the application form

    Fill out the application form available at the identification office or online.

  4. Step 4: Submit the form and documents

    Submit the completed form along with the required documents to the designated officer.

  5. Step 5: Biometric data capture

    Undergo biometric data capture including fingerprints, photograph, and signature.

  6. Step 6: Receive acknowledgment slip

    Receive an acknowledgment slip with your application number for tracking.

  7. Step 7: Track application status

    Track the status of your application online using your application number.

  8. Step 8: Collect the National ID card

    Collect your National ID card upon notification from the identification office.
